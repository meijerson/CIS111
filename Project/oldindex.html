You CAN Do This!

Are you a parent who has never played piano before but would like your children to learn how to play? Can you not afford to pay for their lessons at this time in your life? You do not need to be musically inclined, just follow our simple instructions and you will have beautiful music being played in your home in no time.

Piano tip of the week!

When teaching a very young piano student for the first time, they may not know their right hand from their left. Put a sticker on each one of their hands - "L" for left, and "R" for right!

Soon they will remember which hand is which without the stickers. If you do not have stickers, with the parent's permission you can write the letters on their hand.
